# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free and inclusive for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers.
